About

Jayson Orense is an autodidact photographer based in the Philippines. His photography is driven by a fascination with the moments that surround us. His work mostly consists of moments of everyday life that are hidden in plain sight.

His goal as a photographer is to document and bring out the beauty in ordinary scenes. In July of 2021, Jayson launched this website, which he built from scratch, where he began exhibiting his work and growth in photography.
